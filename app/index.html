What can you do with Big Data

The main challenge of big data analytics is to find the right data to present.

Because of the sheer volume and variety of the information available, it is very difficult to know where interesting data lie and how that data is related to other data sources.

Visual query languages help data scientists formulate the right question and hence narrow down on the right data.

The idea is to use pre-generated database statistics to build an exploratory graph that allows users to visually navigate complex datasets.

Declutter Information. It'll blow your mind.

The relative size of each node, together with its connections with other nodes, will serve as indicators of interesting segments within the datasets. As the user expands deeper into the each node, pig queries are composed on the fly and new visualisations are created in real time as results arrive.
